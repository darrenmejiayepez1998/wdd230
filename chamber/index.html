    DVD Game Exchange

    The best local stop for any movies or video games you are looking for! This store has been locally owned for years and has a wide collection of content available for purchase. Plus, they often have special deals and promotions that you won't want to miss out on!

    TRUreligion Pancake and Steakhouse

    This hot new spot is the perfect place to go with your friends or family when you want to relax and enjoy a delicious, professionally made breakfast. With its cozy ambiance and friendly staff, you'll feel right at home as you savor each bite. They offer a diverse menu featuring both classic breakfast favorites and innovative dishes, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you're craving fluffy pancakes, savory omelets, or freshly brewed coffee, this restaurant has it all. Don't miss out on their weekend brunch specials, where you can indulge in mouthwatering treats while catching up with loved ones.

    University Place Mall

    The go-to mall in all of Utah County, this shopping center is renowned for its wide array of popular stores and unbeatable deals. Whether you're searching for the latest fashion trends, electronics, or home goods, you'll find it all under one roof. With its convenient location and ample parking, it's the perfect destination for a day of shopping with friends or family. Additionally, the mall hosts exciting events and promotions throughout the year, providing even more reasons to visit. From holiday sales to exclusive pop-up shops, there's always something new to discover at this bustling retail hub.
