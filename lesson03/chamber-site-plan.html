Site Name

Orem Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Orem Chamber of Commerce's main mission is to promote newly opened businesses, especially those starting from the ground up, and provide networking opportunities for small business owners to grow.

Scenarios

How can I get help in growing and promoting my buisness?

What are the benefits of becoming a chamber member?

How have some local buisnesses benefited from being chamber members?

Color Schema Plan

  • Orange: Heading and footer background to create a sense of warmth, ambition and enthusiasm towards helping local buisnesses.
  • White: White for the background color in the body of the website with a black color on the text font.

These colors represent what the chambers mission of promoting and helping grow other buisnesses represents as well as creating a visually appealing website.

Typography plan

  • Font for Headings: Pacifico
  • Font for body: Signika Negative

These fonts are soft yet convey a 'go-getter' attitude. They are similar to those used by companies like Chick-Fil-A and capture the spirit of what the site aims to accomplish. Not to mention, they are very readable.
